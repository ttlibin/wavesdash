Contact Us

We'd love to hear from you! Whether you have questions, feedback, suggestions, or need support, we're here to help. Please reach out to us through any of the following methods:

For general inquiries, feedback, or support, please feel free to contact us. We typically respond within 24-48 hours.

For questions about our games, platform features, or general information about WavesDash, please contact us through our website.

Report Issues

If you encounter any technical issues, broken games, or problems with the website, please report them to us with as much detail as possible, including:

  • The game or page where the issue occurred
  • Your device and browser information
  • A description of the problem

Suggestions & Feedback

We value your input! If you have suggestions for new games, features, or improvements, we'd love to hear from you. Your feedback helps us make WavesDash better for everyone.

Copyright & DMCA

If you believe that any content on WavesDash infringes your copyright, please contact us with:

  • A description of the copyrighted work

  • Your contact information
  • A statement of good faith belief

Response Time

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. Thank you for your patience!
